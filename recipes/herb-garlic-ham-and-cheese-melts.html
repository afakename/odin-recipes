Herb-Garlic Ham & Cheese Melts

When you want a savory snack to feed a family during the holidays, this perfect finger food will be sure to please. I first had these at a Christmas gathering and it was so amazing that I almost ate the entire pan. Even after having a full sampler plate of everything else being served. The five flavors put together make for a mouth watering melty happy dance.

These little sliders can be made really quick and are just the perfect little meal or even could qualify as a light snack. It is highly suggested you add this to your next potluck.

Ingredients

  1. 12 oz. thinly sliced Black Forest ham
  2. 6 oz. thinly sliced white Cheddar cheese
  3. 5.2 oz. container semisoft cheese with garlic and herbs
  4. 6 Tbsp. butter, melted
  5. 2 Tbsp. finely chopped shallot
  6. 1 Tbsp. finely chopped parsley
  7. 1 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
  8. 1 clove minced garlic

Let's get snacking!!

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine the butter, shallot, parsley, mustard and garlic in a bowl.
  3. Arrange bottoms of rolls in a 13Ã—9-inch baking pan or 3-quart rectangular baking dish.
  4. Add filling and tops of rolls.
  5. Drizzle the combined liquid over sandwiches.
  6. Cover pan with foil.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes at 350.
  8. Remove foild and bake an additional 10 to 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted and roll tops are light brown.
